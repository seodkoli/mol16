Frequently Asked Questions

Can I replace a single panel of my garage door?

In most cases, yes, a single panel can be replaced without replacing the entire door, but it's important to ensure that the replacement panel matches the style and dimensions of the existing door.

How do I know if my garage door needs repair?

Signs that your garage door needs repair include unusual noises, slow operation, uneven movement, or visible damage to the door or its components.

How often should I maintain my garage door?

It's recommended to perform maintenance checks on your garage door annually, including lubricating moving parts and inspecting for any signs of wear or damage.
