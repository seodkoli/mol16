What safety features should I look for in a garage door opener?

Look for garage door openers with features such as automatic reversal mechanisms, infrared sensors to detect obstructions, and rolling code technology for enhanced security.

What are the common garage door materials?

Common materials for garage doors include steel, wood, aluminum, and fiberglass.

What is the difference between torsion and extension springs?

Torsion springs are mounted above the garage door opening and wind up to store energy, while extension springs are mounted on either side of the door and stretch to provide lifting force.
